BQHS Resources

We compiled this Biostatistics and Quantitative Health Sciences (BQHS) resource page for your convenience. For online national or Hawaiʻi state statistical data resources, please refer to JABSOM Health Science Library Statistical Data Sources. The following links focus on basic QHS tools and methods. BQHS strongly recommends that researchers contact a professional biostatistician before embarking on a research study and/or data analysis project.

Disclaimer: All data and information on this webpage are believed to be accurate and come from sources believed to be reliable. BQHS can not guarantee that the data and information linked through this page are accurate, up-to-date, or complete. User discretion advised.

Statistical Calculators

P-value Calculator

For a sample from a population with a known proabability distribution, a p-value can be calculated an online calculator. The program was developed by Dr. Hossein Arsham of the University of Baltimore.

Online P-value Calculator


Power and Sample Size Calculators

Power and sampel size calculation is crucial to properly plan your staudy and control your budget. Several JavaScript-based calculators are available from the following universities:

University of California, San Francisco University of Iowa


Note: Power and sample size calculation is intended to help design a study. It is not intended to be conducted after the data has already been collected. We highly recommend that researchers who are planning for a study consult a professional biostatistician well before the initiation of the study.

NCBI CDD

MGI: Mouse Genome Informatics

Database and bioinformatics resource on the genetics, genomics, and biology of mouse

Database of tissue-specific gene expression and regulation data

Database of normal, pre-cancerous and cancerous genomes

RCSB Protein Databank

Repository of 3D structures of large biological molecules, including proteins and nucleic acids

ExPASy
