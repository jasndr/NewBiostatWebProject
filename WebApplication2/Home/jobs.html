Looking for Exciting Opportunities in Paradise?

BQHS is always looking for qualified and self-driven Ph.D. and Master-level biostatisticians, bioinformaticians, programmers, and database specialists to join our growing team. The following are summaries of their designated job descriptions. Please see corresponding links for full job description information.

Bioinformatician Positions

Ph.D. Bioinformatician
  • Duties & Responsibilities: Use existing bioinformatics software or develop new approaches to solve problems during the conduct of biomedical research and to gain a better understanding of complex biological activities.
  • Minimum Qualifications: Ph.D. Degree in Bioinformatics, Biostatistics, Computer Sciences, or related quantitative fields & one (1) year of translational bioinformatics collaboration in an academic/biotech setting.
M.S. Bioinformatician
  • Duties & Responsibilities: Use existing bioinformatics software to support biomedical research projects under general guidance.
  • Minimum Qualifications: Master's Degree in Bioinformatics, Biostatistics, Computer Sciences, or related quantitative fields & one (1) year of translational bioinformatics collaboration in an academic/biotech setting.

Biostatistician Positions

Senior Ph.D. Biostatistician
  • Duties & Responsibilities: Independently provide research design and statistical analysis support to diverse research projects.
  • Minimum Qualifications: Ph.D. Degree in Biostatistics, Statistics, or related quantitative fields & at least five (5) years of post-doctoral collaborative research and consultation experience.
Ph.D. Biostatistician
  • Duties & Responsibilities: Provide research design and statistical analysis support to diverse research projects under general guidance.
  • Minimum Qualifications: Ph.D. Degree in Biostatistics, Statistics, or related quantitative fields & experience interacting with multiple biomedical research investigators and providing statistical support.
Senior M.S. Biostatistician
  • Duties & Responsibilities: Independently provide consultation and collaboration support on research design and statistical analysis to multiple research projects under minimal guidance.
  • Minimum Qualifications: Master's Degree in Biostatistics or Statistics & at least three (3) years of full-time, post-graduate statistical programming and data analysis experience.
M.S. Biostatistician/Data Analyst
  • Duties & Responsibilities: Provide statistical programming and analysis support to multiple research projects.
  • Minimum Qualifications: Master's Degree in Biostatistics & experience in interacting with biomedical researchers, and providing statistical programming and analysis support.

Technical Positions

Clinical Database Programmer/Analyst
  • Duties & Responsibilities: Work closely with BQHS staff and research collaborators to develop and maintain databases for clinical trials and clinical research studies.
  • Minimum Qualifications: Bachelor's Degree in Computer Sciences or related field & at least three years of full-time experience as a database programmer/analyst in a biomedical research setting or pharmaceutical/biotech/CRO industry with demonstrated professional maturity.

If you have read the full job description links by clicking the individual job titles above and are interested in exploring career opportunities at UH JABSOM BQHS, please submit a cover letter describing your interest and qualifications, your resume/CV, and a copy of school transcripts via email to qhsjobs@hawaii.edu.
