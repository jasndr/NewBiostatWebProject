Results Dissemination

The BQHS faculty and staff have extensive experience with results presentation and dissemination, such as:

  • Biostatistics and bioinformatics results interpretation
  • Results presentations (e.g., figures, tables) for manuscripts and posters
  • Biostatistics and bioinformatics methodology and results sections development for reports and manuscripts

Note: Authorship is generally expected on scientific journal articles or presentations, where substantial efforts on research design and/or data analysis are provided. Reimbursement for BQHS support (either through %FTE or fee-for-service) does not preclude or replace authorship.
