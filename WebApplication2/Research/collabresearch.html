Collaborative Research


Research has become increasingly more interdisciplinary and requires partnering with other departments, disciplines, and institutions. Our faculty and staff have experience working with collaborative research projects and welcome the development of new collaborations.

Some of our current collaborators include:

JABSOM Departments & Centers

  • Department of Medicine
  • Department of Obstetrics, Gynecology, and Women's Health
  • P20 Center for Native and Pacific Health Disparities Research
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging Center

UHM Schools & Colleges

  • School of Nursing & Dental Hygiene
  • College of Tropical Agriculture and Human Resources

Non-UH Organizations

  • Queen's Medical Center
  • Shriners Hospitals for Children
  • Hawaii Health Information Corporation
  • Hawaii Journal of Medicine & Public Health

For academic groups or organizations with long-term projects, a collaborative agreement can be established with BQHS, which is a cost-effective and efficient approach to ensure comprehensive and long-term analytic support for research activities. The BQHS faculty and staff can support your long-term project through either percentage of full-time equivalent (FTE) salary recovery or a negotiated service contract. If you are interested in establishing a collaborative agreement for quantitative health sciences support, please contact us at biostat@hawaii.edu or (808) 692-1840.
