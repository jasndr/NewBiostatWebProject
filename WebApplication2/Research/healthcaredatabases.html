Healthcare Databases


Healthcare Database

Large health databases are useful resources for institutional and community healthcare researchers. The databases, if utilized properly, can be a low-cost alternative in generating preliminary data, developing research hypothesis, or for education and training purposes. Many community health researchers lack the resources and/or skills to access and properly handle large databases. BQHS has been developing an infrastructure resource of several major public-use databases, providing data management and analysis support, and developing methodological research using the healthcare databases.

BQHS provides guidance on the data and its usage, including proper methods for working with complex survey weights. To encourage the use of healthcare databases, BQHS is in the process of making a catalog of possible data resources available on our website, including data sources. In addition, BQHS will create a library of examples of analyses using data from various sources. The objective is to make the greatest number of data sources known and available to healthcare researchers in Hawaii.
