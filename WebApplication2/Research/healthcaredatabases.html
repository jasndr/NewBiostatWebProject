Healthcare Databases


Healthcare Databases

Large healthcare databases are useful resources for clinical and translational researchers and community-based researchers. The databases, if utilized properly, can be a low-cost alternative in generating preliminary data, developing research hypothesis, or for training and educational purposes. BQHS has been developing an infrastructure resource for several large public use databases, providing data management and analysis support, and developing methodological research using the healthcare databases.

BQHS provides guidance on the data and its usage, including proper methods for working with complex survey weights. To encourage the use of healthcare databases by healthcare researchers in Hawaii, BQHS has a catalog of possible data resources available on our web site . In addition, BQHS will create a library of examples of analyses using data from the various sources.
