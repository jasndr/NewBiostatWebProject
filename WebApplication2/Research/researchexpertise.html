Research Strengths


The faculty and staff at BQHS aspire to be a crossroads of knowledge from the biological, physical, and social sciences, developing a holistic understanding of the health of populations and creating viable solutions to their problems by developing a sound design for their studies, determining an optimal sample size, and developing a comprehensive data analysis plan that reflects their study design and specific aims. BQHS continues to advance and develop statistical methods for the data analysis of large scale healthcare datasets, and next generation sequencing technologies.

Research Areas of Strength

  • Clinical Trial/Clinical Research
  • Epidemiology
  • Longitudinal and Hierarchical Data Analysis
  • Multivariate Analysis
  • Spatial Statistics
  • Bioinformatics and Computational Biology
  • Biomedical Informatics
  • Healthcare Data Analytics
  • Survival Analysis
  • Structural Equational Modeling
  • Data Visualization
  • Nonparametric Analysis
  • Bayesian Biostatistics
