BQHS Resources

Free Grant Development and Preparation

To encourage and facilitate more research activities at JABSOM and its affiliated institutes, BQHS will provide support and expertise in the developement and writing of extramural research grant proposals, excluding extensive preliminary data analysis effort, free of charge as long as adequate precentage FTE of the participating faculty and staff is built into the grant budget. If extensive pilot data analysis is needed, the BQHS faculty and/or staff can serve as co-PI (Principal Investigators) and/or co-investigators in the grant proposal, with adequate FTE salary recovery (usually a minimum of 5% FTE). As grant application deadlines tend to be clustered around certain dates, please contact us well before the deadline so we will be able to properly support your application and increase your chance of obtaining funding.

Long-Term Funded Projects

For extramural and intramural funded research projects, BQHS faculty and staff can support your study through either percentage FTE salary recovery or a negotiated service contract.

Short-Term or Non-Extramurally Funded Projects

On a fee-for-service basis, faculty and staff can provide research design and analysis services to self-initiated projects supported by the investigator's department funds or other sources.

The current BQHS rates (2016-2017) for UH or UH-affiliated projects are:

  • $120/hr for Ph.D.-level Faculty Experts
  • $60/hr for Master's-level Staff Analysts

For non-UH clients, rates will be negotiated individually.
